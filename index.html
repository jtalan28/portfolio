About Pelagic Beach Resort

Welcome to Pelagic Beach Resort, your serene seaside retreat tucked away in Pagkilatan, Batangas City. With its picturesque coastline and laid-back ambiance, Pelagic offers the perfect getaway for families, friends, or couples wanting to escape the hustle and bustle.

What We Offer

  • Spacious swimming pools perfect for families
  • Basketball court for group activities and team building
  • Beachfront access and scenic views
  • Comfortable cottages & well-maintained rooms
